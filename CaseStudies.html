Aussie Adventures is made for travel inspiration in the 'new normal'.
Friends and family come together and share the places they can't wait to see... when we're finally allowed.
Aussie Adventures provides a gallery of inspirational places to visit, with users' own photos, locations and essential travel data.

CommunityConnect is an online app, connecting people who need help with volunteers in their community.
Users can post tasks or requests on the site, to show they need help. Other users can then view available tasks and accept them.

Worldwide Weather is the essential travel tool for anyone planning a trip.
It provides all the key weather information for any city in the world, as well as a handy five-day forecast.
For the seasoned traveller, it allows users to save locations they regularly visit, and see the information at the click of a button.
